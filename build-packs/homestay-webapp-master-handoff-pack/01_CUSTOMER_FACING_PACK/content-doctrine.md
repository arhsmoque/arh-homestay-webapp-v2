# Content Doctrine

```text
Proof before adjective.
Situation before slogan.
Action before ornament.
Rules visible, not frightening.
Social-aware, not slang-led.
Malay-English compatible, not randomly mixed.
```

## Bad

```text
A luxurious peaceful escape perfect for everyone.
```

## Better

```text
Private pool homestay near [Location] for family weekends, BBQ, family day, and small private events. Sleeps up to X with parking for X cars.
```
