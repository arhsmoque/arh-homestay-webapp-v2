# Customer-Facing Design Brief

## Ought Statement

```yaml
ought_statement:
  must_make_possible:
    - Guest understands property fit quickly.
    - Guest can evaluate pax, rooms, pool, parking, layout, location, packages, and rules.
    - Guest can send a structured inquiry through WhatsApp.
    - Public pages can be searched, shared, and understood by Google/social platforms.
    - Future booking, admin, payment, and analytics can attach without rewriting the public foundation.

  must_prevent:
    - Photo dump without decision value.
    - Generic luxury slogans without proof.
    - F&B menu/cart metaphor leaking into homestay booking.
    - Guest confusion between stay, event, family day, and corporate retreat.
    - Hidden rules or unclear rate factors.
    - Heavy app shell hurting public performance.

  must_reveal:
    - Property location and access.
    - Sleeping pax vs event pax.
    - Private pool details and rules.
    - Parking and layout.
    - Package suitability.
    - Booking/inquiry next step.

  must_preserve:
    - Public performance.
    - Searchability.
    - Shareability.
    - Media proof.
    - Future operator integration seams.
```
