emotional_keywords:
  - warm

## Visual keywords

```yaml
visual_keywords:
  - warm off-white canvas
  - pool teal accents
  - clay / terracotta warmth
  - rattan / sand neutrals
  - palm / olive greens
  - soft shadows
  - real photo proof
  - slow motion
  - spacious rhythm
  - clear CTAs
```

## Avoid

```yaml
avoid:
  - black-gold luxury
  - generic corporate blue
  - neon AI palette
  - sterile gray SaaS
  - fake villa render look
  - overly sharp AI-upscaled photo feel
  - heavy glassmorphism
  - autoplay chaos
```

## Design ought statement

```yaml
frontend_ought_statement:
  must_make_possible:
    - "A visitor feels the place is real and trustworthy within seconds."
    - "A family can imagine staying there without reading long copy."
    - "An event planner can understand usability, not just beauty."
    - "Photos, cards, copy, and motion work together as proof."
    - "The public site remains fast and comfortable on mobile."

  must_prevent:
    - "A beautiful but vague homestay brochure."
    - "AI-polished visuals that make the property look fake."
    - "Motion that distracts from booking."
    - "Gallery/video bloat that damages performance."

  must_reveal:
    - private_pool
    - space_rooms_parking_layout
    - event_usability
    - day_night_rain_ambience
    - clear_next_action

  must_preserve:
    - warm_hospitality
    - proof_led_clarity
    - mobile_performance
    - accessibility
    - future_token_control
```
