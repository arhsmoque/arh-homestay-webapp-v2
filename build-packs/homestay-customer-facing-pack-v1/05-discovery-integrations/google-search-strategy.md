# Google Search Strategy

## Purpose

Google Search captures high-intent visitors already looking for a place.

## Target Queries

- homestay private pool [location]
- private pool homestay near [landmark]
- homestay for family day [location]
- homestay for gathering [location]
- villa with private pool [location]
- Muslim friendly homestay [location]
- homestay near [hospital/university/airport/town]

## Page Strategy

Each page has one discovery job:

- `/` — main entity and brand
- `/private-pool` — pool intent
- `/stay` — family stay and sleeping proof
- `/events` — event / family day / retreat intent
- `/layout` — private compound and parking trust proof
- `/location` — landmark and map discovery
- `/packages` — commercial intent
- `/faq` — long-tail objection capture

## Metadata Template
