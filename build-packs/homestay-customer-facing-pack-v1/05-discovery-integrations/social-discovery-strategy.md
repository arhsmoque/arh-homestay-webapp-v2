# Social Discovery Strategy

## Flywheel

```text
TikTok / Instagram / Facebook
  ↓
Creates desire and curiosity

Google Search
  ↓
Captures intent

Google Maps / Business Profile
  ↓
Confirms location, reviews, photos, legitimacy

Website
  ↓
Proves fit: pax, pool, rooms, parking, layout, packages, rules

WhatsApp
  ↓
Captures structured inquiry

Stay / event
  ↓
Creates review and social proof

Review / repost
  ↓
Feeds Google, Maps, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram
```

## TikTok Role

TikTok is a desire engine, not a full booking system.

Content pillars:

- Private pool
- Night swim
- Rain/evening mood
- Family day setup
- Compound layout tour
- Route / gate / parking
- FAQ clips

Landing routes:

```yaml
pool_video: /private-pool?tiktok=pool-night
layout_video: /layout?tiktok=compound-tour
family_day_video: /events?tiktok=family-day
route_video: /location?tiktok=route
package_video: /packages?tiktok=weekday-retreat
```

## Instagram / Facebook Role

Instagram:

- visual proof
- reels
- carousels
- ambience

Facebook:

- family/community sharing
- local trust
- event posts
- review posts
- availability announcements

## Embed Policy

Build now:

- curated social proof cards
- selected thumbnails with links
- social profile links
- share previews

Avoid for MVP:

- full live feeds
- heavy third-party embeds
- autoplay social videos
