# Customer-Facing Homestay Webapp Design Brief

## Product

**Homestay Guest Decision Engine**

A public-facing system that helps guests understand whether the property fits their family stay, private pool visit, family day, small event, ceremony, or corporate retreat, then converts that understanding into a structured WhatsApp inquiry.

## Core Thesis

The site should not merely show the homestay. It should help the right guest prove that the property fits their situation.

```text
Old:
Show the homestay.

New:
Help the right guest decide whether this is the right stay or event space.
```

## Public Spine

```text
Desire
→ Fit
→ Proof
→ Logistics
→ Occasion
→ Trust
→ Action
```

## Reject for MVP

- F&B menu/order metaphor
- Generic hotel booking grid
- Heavy public SPA
- Login-first booking
- Autoplay-heavy social video
- Full social feed embeds
- Payment before availability confirmation
- Hidden rules until after deposit
- Vague “DM for price” with no rate factors

## Final Acceptance

The customer-facing site is acceptable when:

1. A first-time visitor understands the property within 3 seconds.
2. A serious visitor can judge fit within 30 seconds.
3. Pool, pax, parking, layout, location, packages, and rules are visible before inquiry.
4. Stay, event, family day, and corporate use cases are separated.
5. WhatsApp inquiry is structured and purpose-specific.
6. The site is fast, accessible, searchable, shareable, and future-adapter-ready.
