# Homepage Components

## 1. HeroDecisionCard

Purpose: answer what, where, who for, main hook, and next action.

Fields:

- headline
- subheadline
- hero media
- proof chips
- primary CTA
- secondary CTA
- tertiary CTA

## 2. FastProofStrip

Purpose: no-hunt hard facts.

Items:

- Sleeps X
- X rooms
- Private pool
- Parking X cars
- Kitchen
- Near [landmark]

## 3. OccasionFitCard

Purpose: route by real guest situation.

Cards:

- Family Weekend
- Family Day
- Private Event
- Small Corporate Retreat

## 4. SignaturePoolCard

Purpose: prove the strongest emotional/search hook.

Facts:

- Private use
- Day/night/rain photos
- Pool rules
- Children supervised

## 5. SleepSummaryCard / RoomSleepCard

Purpose: clarify sleeping pax and room fit.

Separate:

- comfortable sleeping pax
- maximum sleeping pax
- event pax

## 6. CompoundLayoutPreview

Purpose: make the space understandable.

Labels:

- main house
- guest house
- pool
- parking
- gazebo
- pergola
- gate
- BBQ / event area

## 7. EventSuitabilityCard

Purpose: clarify family day, birthday, akad, engagement, corporate retreat suitability.

## 8. GalleryStoryCard

Purpose: grouped visual proof.

Groups:

- pool
- rooms
- living
- kitchen
- parking
- day
- night
- rain
- event setup

## 9. LocationProofCard

Purpose: map, nearby places, and arrival confidence.

## 10. PackageSummaryCard

Purpose: commercial framing by purpose.

## 11. ReviewProofCard / FaqPreviewCard

Purpose: reduce anxiety before inquiry.

## 12. WhatsappInquiryCard

Purpose: convert into structured message.
