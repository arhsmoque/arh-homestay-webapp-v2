# Non-Goals

## Not an Airbnb clone

Airbnb optimizes marketplace comparison. This site optimizes direct trust and direct inquiry.

## Not a hotel brochure

Hotels sell standardized rooms. A homestay sells property fit, privacy, layout, occasion suitability, and local trust.

## Not an F&B menu site

F&B users choose menu items quickly. Homestay users assess risk, group logistics, dates, rules, budget, and fit.

## Not a photo dump

Photos attract, but layout, pax, rules, parking, pool, packages, and location convert.

## Not booking-engine-first

For owner-operated Malaysian homestay operation, WhatsApp/inquiry may remain the practical first checkout lane.

## Not 3D-showcase-first

3D may help later, but the foundation is property facts, layout, trust proof, media, and booking path.
