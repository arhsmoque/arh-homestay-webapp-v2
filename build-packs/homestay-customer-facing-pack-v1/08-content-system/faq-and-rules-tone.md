# FAQ and Rules Tone

## FAQ Categories

- Stay
- Pool
- Event
- Parking
- Booking
- Location

## FAQ Answer Pattern

```text
Answer directly.
Add condition if needed.
Point to next action.
```

Example:

```text
Q: Is the pool private?

A: Yes. The pool is for your group during your booking. Children must be supervised and pool rules apply.
```

Example:

```text
Q: Can we do family day?

A: Yes, family day may be arranged depending on date, pax, and setup. Please declare event use before confirmation so we can advise the correct rate and rules.
```

## Rules Tone

Bad:

```text
Guests who violate any rules will be penalized immediately. Management is not responsible for anything.
```

Better:

```text
To keep the space comfortable and safe for all guests, please follow the house, pool, and event rules. If you are planning an event, please declare it before confirmation.
```
