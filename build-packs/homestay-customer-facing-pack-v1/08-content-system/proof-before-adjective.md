# Proof Before Adjective

## Rule

For every adjective, provide proof.

## Instead of "peaceful"

Use:

- private compound
- away from busy shared facilities
- suitable for family rest and pool time

## Instead of "luxury"

Use:

- private pool
- night ambience
- covered gathering area
- comfortable rooms
- clean, prepared space

## Instead of "spacious"

Use:

- parking for X cars
- event pax up to X
- separate pool and gathering zones
- main house + guest house

## Instead of "strategic"

Use:

- X minutes to [landmark]
- near [town/road]
- Google Maps pin available
- food and shops nearby

## Instead of "family-friendly"

Use:

- private pool
- kitchen
- rooms for X pax
- parking
- children must be supervised at pool

## Instead of "event-ready"

Use:

- day event pax up to X
- parking for X cars
- covered area
- BBQ / food setup area
- event rules apply
